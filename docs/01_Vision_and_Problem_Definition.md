No molecule-first, SA-native source of truth. Global tools (UpToDate, Epocrates, Drugs.com) are excellent and foreign; local tools (MIMS SA, Snyman's, the SAHPRA register) are authoritative but siloed, brand-first, and built for lookup, not understanding.
